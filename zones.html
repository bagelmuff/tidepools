zones in tide pools

splash zone

The highest part of the shore, only ever touched by ocean spray. it's almost never underwater.

upper intertidal

This zone gets covered briefly during high tides but spends most of the day in open air.

mid intertidal

Covered and uncovered twice every day as the tides come in and go out.

low intertidal

The lowest zone, only exposed during the very lowest tides.

Each creature has a very specific zone it lives in.
